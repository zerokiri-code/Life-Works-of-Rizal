Community Works

Education

Rizal founded a school where he taught young boys subjects like reading, writing, Spanish, math, and moral values...

Healthcare

Rizal treated the sick using his medical expertise, especially in ophthalmology. He offered his services freely to the poor and performed surgeries, contributing greatly to the health of the local population during his exile in Dapitan.

Agriculture

Rizal improved farming practices by introducing modern techniques and tools. He cultivated his own land and taught others to be self-sufficient, helping uplift the economic state of Dapitan through sustainable agriculture.

Architech

Rizal's architectural contributions during his exile in Dapitan reflect his eye for design, utility, and the harmonious blending of his environment with local materials. As an architect, Rizal focused on creating functional, aesthetically pleasing spaces that reflected his values of simplicity and sustainability.

Engineer

During his exile in Dapitan, José Rizal demonstrated his engineering skills by solving practical problems that significantly improved the daily lives of the local community. His work in engineering focused on projects that had immediate, long-term benefits for the town’s infrastructure, public health, and economy.
